# Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I add my first deal?
**A**: Navigate to the Pipeline page and click the "Create Deal" button. Fill in the company name, select the current stage, and add any relevant details. Click "Save" to add the deal to your pipeline.

### Q: Can I import existing data?
**A**: Yes! Go to Settings > Data Import to upload deals and companies via CSV. Download our template to ensure proper formatting.

### Q: How do I invite team members?
**A**: Admins can invite team members from Settings > Team. Enter their email address, select their role (Admin, Partner, or Analyst), and click "Send Invitation."

## Deals & Pipeline

### Q: What are the default deal stages?
**A**: The default stages are: Inbound → Screening → First Meeting → Deep Dive → Due Diligence → IC Review → Term Sheet → Closed (or Passed). Admins can customize these in Settings.

### Q: Can I customize the deal stages?
**A**: Yes, admins can add, remove, or rename stages in Settings > Pipeline Configuration.

### Q: What happens when I archive a deal?
**A**: Archived deals are removed from your active pipeline but preserved for reporting. You can restore them anytime from the Archive.

## AI Agents

### Q: What are AI agents?
**A**: AI agents are automated tools that use artificial intelligence to perform tasks like deal screening, market research, and portfolio monitoring.

### Q: Is my data safe with AI agents?
**A**: Yes. We use enterprise-grade AI services with strict data protection. Your data is not used to train models.

### Q: Can I customize AI agents?
**A**: Yes, admins can configure agent settings, adjust criteria, and customize outputs in the Agents section.

## Portfolio & Analytics

### Q: How is IRR calculated?
**A**: IRR (Internal Rate of Return) is calculated based on the dates and amounts of capital calls and distributions. We use the XIRR method for accuracy.

### Q: How often is portfolio data updated?
**A**: Real-time for deal and company updates. Valuations and metrics update based on your configuration.

## Security & Privacy

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A**: Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure, and regular security audits.

### Q: Who can see my deal data?
**A**: Only users you invite to your team can access your data. Role-based permissions control what each user can see and do.

## Billing & Account

### Q: What plans are available?
**A**: We offer Starter (up to 3 users), Professional (up to 10 users), and Enterprise (unlimited) plans.

### Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
**A**: Admins can cancel from Settings > Billing. You'll have access until the end of your current billing period.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Why can't I log in?
**A**: Check that you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link.

### Q: The page isn't loading properly. What should I do?
**A**: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use an incognito window. Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
