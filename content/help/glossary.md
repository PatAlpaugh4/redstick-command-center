# VC Terminology Glossary

## Deal Stages

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Inbound** | Initial contact or deal submitted to the firm |
| **Screening** | Initial review of deal to assess fit with investment criteria |
| **First Meeting** | Initial call or meeting with founding team |
| **Deep Dive** | Detailed analysis and due diligence phase |
| **Due Diligence** | Comprehensive investigation of company financials, legal, and operations |
| **IC Review** | Investment Committee presentation and decision |
| **Term Sheet** | Non-binding agreement outlining investment terms |
| **Closed** | Investment completed, capital deployed |
| **Passed** | Deal declined or withdrawn |

## Funding Stages

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Pre-Seed** | Earliest funding, often from angels or accelerators ($0-$1M) |
| **Seed** | First institutional funding round ($1M-$3M) |
| **Series A** | Product-market fit achieved, scaling operations ($3M-$15M) |
| **Series B** | Scaling go-to-market and team ($15M-$50M) |
| **Series C** | Expansion and market dominance ($50M+) |
| **Growth** | Late-stage funding for established companies |

## Financial Metrics

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **IRR** | Internal Rate of Return: Annualized effective compounded return rate |
| **TVPI** | Total Value to Paid-In: (Distributions + NAV) / Paid-In Capital |
| **DPI** | Distribution to Paid-In: Distributions / Paid-In Capital |
| **RVPI** | Residual Value to Paid-In: NAV / Paid-In Capital |
| **MOIC** | Multiple on Invested Capital: Total Value / Invested Capital |
| **NAV** | Net Asset Value: Current value of portfolio holdings |
| **Gross IRR** | IRR before management fees and carried interest |
| **Net IRR** | IRR after all fees and carry |

## VC Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **LP** | Limited Partner: Investors in the fund (institutions, family offices) |
| **GP** | General Partner: Fund managers who make investment decisions |
| **Dry Powder** | Committed capital not yet invested |
| **Capital Call** | Request for committed capital from LPs |
| **Distribution** | Cash returned to LPs from portfolio exits |
| **Management Fee** | Annual fee (typically 2%) on committed capital |
| **Carried Interest** | GP's share of profits (typically 20%) |
| **Hurdle Rate** | Minimum return threshold before carry is paid (typically 8%) |
| **Clawback** | Provision requiring GP to return excess carry |
| **Tag-Along** | Right to sell shares if founder sells |
| **Drag-Along** | Obligation to sell if majority shareholders sell |
| **Pro-rata** | Right to invest in future rounds to maintain ownership |
| **Preemptive Rights** | Right to participate in future funding rounds |
| **Information Rights** | Access to company financial and operational data |
| **Board Seat** | Right to appoint a director to company board |
| **Protective Provisions** | Veto rights over specific company actions |

## Platform Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Deal Flow** | Pipeline of investment opportunities |
| **Sourcing** | Finding and generating investment opportunities |
| **Screening** | Initial evaluation of investment opportunities |
| **Portfolio Company** | Company in which the fund has invested |
| **Follow-on** | Additional investment in existing portfolio company |
| **Exit** | Liquidity event (IPO, acquisition, secondary sale) |
| **Bridge Round** | Interim financing between major rounds |
| **Down Round** | Funding at lower valuation than previous round |
| **Up Round** | Funding at higher valuation than previous round |
| **Flat Round** | Funding at same valuation as previous round |
