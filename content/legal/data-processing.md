# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

## Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between Redstick Ventures ("Processor") and the customer ("Controller") using the Redstick Command Center platform.

## Definitions

- **Controller**: The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
- **Processor**: Redstick Ventures, which processes personal data on behalf of the Controller
- **Data Subject**: An identified or identifiable natural person
- **Personal Data**: Any information relating to a Data Subject
- **Processing**: Any operation performed on Personal Data

## Scope and Purpose

### Processing Activities
Redstick Ventures will process Personal Data as necessary to:
- Provide the Redstick Command Center Service
- Maintain and improve the Service
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

### Categories of Data
We may process:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Professional information (company, role)
- Account credentials
- Usage data
- Communications

### Categories of Data Subjects
- Platform users (VC firm employees)
- Portfolio company contacts (with Controller's authorization)
- LP contacts (with Controller's authorization)

## Processor Obligations

### Confidentiality
We ensure all personnel processing Personal Data are subject to confidentiality obligations.

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures as described in our Security Overview.

### Subprocessors
We may engage subprocessors to provide the Service. Current subprocessors include:
- Amazon Web Services (hosting)
- Google Cloud Platform (AI services)
- SendGrid (email delivery)
- Twilio (SMS notifications)

We maintain a current list of subprocessors at [URL] and will notify Controller of any changes.

### Data Subject Rights
We will assist Controller in responding to Data Subject requests regarding:
- Access to Personal Data
- Rectification of inaccurate data
- Erasure of data
- Restriction of processing
- Data portability
- Objection to processing

### Data Breach Notification
We will notify Controller within 24 hours of discovering a Personal Data breach and provide:
- Description of the breach
- Categories and approximate number of affected Data Subjects
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken or proposed

### Data Protection Impact Assessment
We will assist Controller with data protection impact assessments as required by applicable law.

## Controller Obligations

Controller warrants that:
- It has a lawful basis for processing Personal Data
- It has provided appropriate notices to Data Subjects
- Processing instructions comply with applicable law
- It has obtained necessary consents or authorizations

## Data Transfer

### International Transfers
Personal Data may be transferred to countries outside the EEA. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Adequacy decisions
- Other legally recognized mechanisms

## Data Retention and Deletion

### Retention Period
We process Personal Data for the duration of the Service agreement and as required by law.

### Return and Deletion
Upon termination:
- We will return or delete Personal Data as instructed by Controller
- We may retain anonymized or aggregated data
- We may retain data as required by law

## Audit Rights

Controller may audit our compliance with this DPA:
- Once annually at no cost
- Additional audits as required by law or regulation
- Subject to reasonable notice and confidentiality obligations

## Liability

Each party's liability is subject to the limitations in the Terms of Service. However, both parties remain liable for their respective obligations under data protection laws.

## Term and Termination

This DPA is effective as of the date Controller begins using the Service and continues until termination of the Terms of Service.

## Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the same law as the Terms of Service.

## Updates

We may update this DPA to comply with legal requirements or reflect changes in our practices. We will notify Controller of material changes.
