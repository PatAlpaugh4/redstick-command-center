## Feature Descriptions

### Deal Pipeline Management
Manage your entire investment pipeline in one visual workspace. From inbound leads to signed term sheets, track every deal through your process with complete context at your fingertips.

**Key capabilities:**
- Kanban board view with customizable stages
- Bulk actions for efficient pipeline management
- Smart filters and saved views
- Deal scoring and prioritization
- Automated stage transitions and notifications
- Document management per deal
- Team collaboration and notes

### Portfolio Analytics
Understand your portfolio performance with comprehensive analytics and reporting. Track the metrics that matter to you and your LPs.

**Key capabilities:**
- Portfolio valuation and performance tracking
- Individual company metrics and milestones
- Sector and geographic breakdowns
- Comparison benchmarking
- Custom dashboard widgets
- Historical trend analysis
- Export to Excel/PDF

### AI-Powered Insights
Let AI handle the repetitive research and analysis so you can focus on decision-making. Our agents work 24/7 to screen deals, monitor markets, and track your portfolio.

**Key capabilities:**
- Deal screening against custom criteria
- Market research and competitive analysis
- Company health monitoring
- News and event tracking
- Automated research memos
- Risk identification and alerts

### LP Reporting
Keep your investors informed with professional, timely reporting. Generate comprehensive reports in minutes and provide 24/7 access through a branded LP portal.

**Key capabilities:**
- Quarterly report generation
- Capital call and distribution notices
