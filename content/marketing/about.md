## Company Story

Redstick Ventures was founded in 2019 by a team of venture capitalists and software engineers who experienced firsthand the challenges of managing deal flow and portfolio data with spreadsheets and generic CRM tools.

We set out to build the platform we wished we had—a tool designed specifically for the unique workflows of venture capital. What started as an internal solution quickly gained attention from other firms facing the same challenges. Today, Redstick Command Center is trusted by hundreds of VC firms worldwide to manage billions in assets.

Our mission extends beyond software. We're building a community of forward-thinking investors who believe that better tools lead to better decisions, and better decisions lead to better outcomes for founders, funds, and the ecosystem as a whole.

## Mission Statement

"Empower venture capital firms with intelligent tools that transform how they discover, evaluate, and support exceptional founders building the future."
