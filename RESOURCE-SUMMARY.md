## 🎯 Key Features Covered

### Kimi K2.5 Capabilities

- ✅ **1T parameters** (32B active per request)
- ✅ **256K context window** (2M long-context)
- ✅ **4 operational modes** (Instant, Thinking, Agent, Agent Swarm)
- ✅ **Agent Swarm** (up to 100 sub-agents)
- ✅ **Multimodal** (text, images, video, documents)
- ✅ **Tool use** (200-300 calls per agent)
- ✅ **Cost efficiency** ($0.60/M input, $2.50/M output)
